An engineer, a mathematician, and a physicist are sitting in a bar. A beautiful woman comes over and says she’ll marry whoever gives her the best definition of pi in seven words.

“Three point one four one five nine,” says the engineer.

The mathematician goes, “Logarithm of negative one, divided by i.”

“Ratio of circumference to diameter,” says the physicist. “I do.”
