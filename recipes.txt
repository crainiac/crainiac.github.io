Spicy Pork
~ 1/4 cup sprite
~ 1/4 cup orange juice
~ 1/3 cup soy sauce
~ 1 tbsp lime juice
~ 1 tbsp sesame oil
~ 1/2 cup gochujang
~ 1 tbsp brown sugar
~ 6 garlic cloves
~ 2 green onions
~ 2 jalapenos, stemmed
~ 2 lbs pork belly, sliced
Blend all ingredients besides pork belly and marinate for 2+ hours.
